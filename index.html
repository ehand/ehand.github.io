About Me

I am a second year Ph.D. student in the Computer Science Department at the University of Maryland, College Park. I received my Bachelor degrees in Computer Science and Engineering and Applied Mathematics at the University of Nevada, Reno in 2013. I worked under the supervision of Dr. George Bebis at the University of Nevada, Dr. Mubarak Shah at the University of Central Florida, and Dr. Ara Nefian at NASA Ames Research center over my four years at the University of Nevada, Reno. Over the summer of 2014, I worked at the NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory with Dr. David Thompson. My advisor at the University of Maryland, College Park is Dr. Rama Chellappa.

I am interested in computer vision and its relationship with both language and common sense reasoning.
